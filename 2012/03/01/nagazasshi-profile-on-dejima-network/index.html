NagaZasshi Profile on Dejima Network!

Posted on: March 1, 2012 by NagaZasshi Editor

We’re used to covering all different kinds of topics in the NagaZasshi – but we’re not so used to becoming the topic ourselves! However, that’s what we became in a recent Dejima Network article. Here, have a look for yourself!

The NagaZasshi editing team was profiled, and editor-in-chief Kim Durinick was interviewed about the behind-the-scenes work needed to make it all tick. Give it a read and go check out the Dejima Network – the international arm of the prefectural government has created a hub for foreigners in or with a tie to Nagasaki, and though it’s still early, it’s got great potential.
