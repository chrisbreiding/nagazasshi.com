AJET Publication Award

Posted on: April 22, 2010 by The NagaZasshi Team

The NagaZasshi Team is proud to announce that we have been honored with an award by AJET. The award is for the best prefectural publication. We went up against some very impressive publications throughout Japan, so, needless to say, we are thrilled to have won the award.

We would like to thank all of our article contributors, our sponsors, and all of you for reading the NagaZasshi. This award inspires us to continue making the best, most informative magazine we possibly can.

Below you can read the email we received from AJET. We’ve posted links to the other publications around Japan on our Links page, so make sure to check those out.

-The NagaZasshi Team

***

Congratulations!

You have won National AJET’s Best Publication Award!

This year there were several strong nominations for each category, with the publications and charity categories particularly competitive. Your certificate will be posted on the 25th of May.

Congratulations and keep up the great work!

Regards,

Andrew Heffernan
National AJET Council, 2009-2010
